Networking & Building Connections

Why Networking Matters

Networking is more than just exchanging contact information—it's about building genuine, long-term professional relationships. Strong connections can help you explore industries, find mentors, and discover job opportunities that aren’t always posted online.

Ways to Build Connections

  • UMSI Alumni Network: Reach out to alumni through Michigan Online Community.
  • LinkedIn: Keep your profile updated and engage with professionals in your field.
  • Career Fairs & Info Sessions: Attend UMSI career events to meet recruiters and learn about employers.
  • Faculty & Peers: Don’t overlook classmates and professors—they can often provide referrals.

Networking Tips

  1. Start small: reach out to alumni or peers with shared interests.
  2. Be authentic: focus on mutual learning instead of just job hunting.
  3. Follow up: send thank-you notes after conversations.
